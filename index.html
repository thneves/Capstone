How it works

THE SEARCH HOUSE WORKS IN FOUR SIMPLE STEPS

1 - SEARCH:

It's simple. On The Search House page, enter the name of the beach or city your looking for a school and click on search, or click on the search link on the top menu to view the list of all schools.

2 - LOCATE:

Get to see where the school is locate by clicking on the school profile. It's even better. You can get the direction from whre you are.

3 - APPLY:

Apply for a surf lesson to the school of your choice. Click on the APPLY button to get the schedule form. We aslo follow up on the application to make sure your lesson is confirmed.

4 - RELAX:

Once you get confirmed, you can now relax at the beach until your lesson time. You can check our blog to see what we recommend about surfing in the area.
